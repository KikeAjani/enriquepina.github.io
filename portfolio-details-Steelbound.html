Description

Steelbound is a 3D adventure game, cooperative both local and online, for two players with an isometric view.

We’ll take control of two lovers, belonging to two opposing sides, they need to escape from Babilonia sharing a unique legendary sword, and thus prevent the war that is coming.

While progressing, they’ll face situations that will require cooperation in an efficient way, adapting the strategy in session in seconds.

Game Features

  • Dynamic roles:

    You have only one sword, you must exchange it continuously to progress. The change of the sword will change your roles in the game.

    Attack on the sword or control the area without it. Mastering the roles will be fundamental.

  • Corruption

    Be careful with hoarding the sword because this will corrupt you and can kill you. Cooperate so that it will not destroy you.

  • Cooperative progression

    You’ll face many challenges, help each other to overcome them, be it enemies or intricate puzzles. Cooperation will make you stronger since the best actions can only be performed with the mixture of both roles' skills.
