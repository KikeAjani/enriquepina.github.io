Steelbound Prototype

  • Style: Co-Op Adventure
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Project date: 19 Mar, 2021
  • Category: University final project
  • Authors: Ishtar Studios
  • Project URL: github.com/danruelop/PrototipoVanishingSword

Description

Prototype of Steelbound to test the experience of the main ideas of Steelbound in-game:

  • Take Sword action
  • Corruption system
  • Brawler and Support interchangeable roles
  • Camera
  • Combat with enemies and boss
  • Puzzles
  • Destructible objects
